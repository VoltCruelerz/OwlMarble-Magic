## Improved Critical (Optional) [New]

_Replaces Brutal Critical_

Beginning at 9th level, you gain increased critical hit range.

| Level | Bonus Dice (Old) | Critical Hit Range (New) |
|:-----:|:----------------:|:------------------------:|
| 9 | 1 | +1 |
| 13 | 2 | +2 |
| 17 | 3 | +3 |
